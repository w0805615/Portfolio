Mobile Web Project

During a assignment, I had the opportunity to demonstrate my proficiency in utilizing the Bootstrap framework to create a website. The task involved emulating a provided design as closely as possible, while incorporating various Bootstrap components and ensuring a responsive and user-friendly experience.

As a novice in working with Bootstrap, this assignment marked the initial phase of my exploration into the framework. I familiarized myself with its core concepts and employed key components such as cards, modals, accordions, and carousels to enhance the website's interactivity and visual appeal.

A primary objective of the project was to ensure responsiveness across different devices, enabling seamless user experiences regardless of the screen size. By leveraging Bootstrap's responsive grid system and implementing appropriate classes, I successfully achieved a layout that adapted smoothly to diverse screen widths.

Web usability Project

During the second semester of my studies, I participated in a project that focused on creating wireframes encompassing lo-fi, hi-fi, and sketch designs. Collaborating with a partner, we divided the workload and embarked on the task of conceptualizing and designing a website for an imaginary client, tailored to their specific requirements.

Our chosen tool for this project was Adobe XD, a versatile software that facilitated the creation of interactive prototypes and simplified the process of sharing our work with stakeholders. Its user-friendly interface and robust features enabled us to effectively communicate our design vision.

The objective of the project was to design a website catering to individuals seeking assistance in planning their trips, including hotel and flight bookings. Imagining ourselves as the client's representatives, we carefully crafted wireframes that showcased recent offers, trending destinations, and exciting travel plans. By focusing on user experience and intuitive design, we aimed to create an engaging platform that inspired and assisted users in organizing their travel arrangements.
